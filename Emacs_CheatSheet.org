* Emacs Cheat Sheet
** undo-tree
- C-x u: undo-tree-visualise (undo-tree-map)
  - C-p: undo / up on tree
  - C-n: redo / down on tree

** redo+
- C-z: undo
- C-M-z: redo

** yasnippet
- yas-expand: C-'
- yas-insert-snippet
- yas-describe-tables

- yas-new-snippet: C-c & n
  - finish editing: C-c C-c

** normal command
- indent-for-tab-command
