** Type
*** BRE
- basic regular expression

**** Meta Characters
***** 置換前
****** External Bracket
******* ^
- 
  head of the sentence

******* $
- 
  end of the sentence

******* .
- 
  a character

******* {...]
- 
  Match a word in the bracket

******* [^...]
- 
  Match other characters in the bracket

******* *
- 
  repeat over 0 times.

******* \{n\}
- 
  n times repeat

******* \{n,\}
- 
  more than n times repeat

******* \{m,n\}
- 
  over m and under n times repeat
